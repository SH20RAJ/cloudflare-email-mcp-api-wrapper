# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Scope

This applies to repository spaces, issues, pull requests, and discussions.
